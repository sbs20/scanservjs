
All options specific to device `magic':
  Scan Mode:
    --mode Lineart|Gray|24bitColor [24bitColor]
        Selects the scan mode (e.g., lineart, monochrome, or color).
    --brightness -50..50 [0]
        Selects the brightness.        
    --contrast -50..50 (in steps of 10) [0]
        Selects the contrast.
    --resolution 75|300|600|1200dpi [75]
        Sets the resolution of the scanned image.
  Geometry:
    -l 0..215.9mm [0]
        Top-left x position of scan area.
    -t 0..297.18mm [0]
        Top-left y position of scan area.
    -x 0..215.9mm [215.9]
        Width of scan-area.
    -y 0..297.18mm [297.18]
        Height of scan-area.
